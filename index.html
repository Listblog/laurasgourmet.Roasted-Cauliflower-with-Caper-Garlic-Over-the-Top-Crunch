Roasted Cauliflower with Caper Garlic Over the Top Crunch Recipe
By Chef Laura Briscoe
Prep time: 15 minutes Cook time: 20 minutes Servings: Serves 2 as a main or 4 as a side

Ingredients

- 1 pound tri colored cauliflower florets

- 1 1/2 Tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil

- 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

- 1 large clove garlic, peeled

- 1 Tablespoon capers, drained and patted dry

- 1/2 cup panko break crumbs (gluten free substitute as needed)

- 2 Tablespoons Over the Top, Original or Italian

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven at 400 degrees Conventional oven or 375 Convection
  2. Massage the cauliflower with olive oil and salt and mix together. Break down any large pieces - they should be bite sized
  3. Place on a baking sheet line with parchment paper. Roast for 17 minutes
  4. While caulflower is roasting, place panko in a large bowl
  5. Pour olive oil in a small saucepan with the clove of garlic. Bring up to heat and simmer.
  6. Once garlic is golden and oil is infused, remove the garlic from the pan
  7. Add capers to same pan and allow to "fry" in olive oil for a few minutes or until crisp
  8. To same pan, add the panko. Toast over medium heat for one minute
  9. Reduce heat and Over the Top blend. Combine well.
  10. Place roasted cauliflower in a large bowl and toss with 3/4 of the Over the Top Caper blend.
  11. Place in a serving bowl and finish with reserved 1/4 of blend and serve
